Schedule

Ticket Registration

For this edition from August 1st to 14th, 10,000 tickets are on sale. An incomparable sociological and cultural event, the 4th edition of Nyeusi will take place at the Oasis Park in Nairobi during the weekend of August 12th, 13th and 14th.

Gates Open

The gate opens for all participants at 12:00 pm on the Friday 12 August, and is open 24 hours a day throughout the event. Plan your arrival so that you show up at the Gate after opening. We cannot allow people who show up early to wait in the parking lot.

Daylight Sessions

Straight from the heart of Nyeusi. A line up of some of the best DJs to take you on a musical journey. Purely chill and sundowner vibes before the party begins.

Age of Bliss

Party all night listening to all your favorite bangers from the top DJs across East Africa. Major performances from artistes like Funk Avenue, GGTheAscendant, Wendy Kay, Zonda Kay and more. Have a night full of bliss and make beautiful memories with us.
