NYEUSI MUSIC
FESTIVAL 2022

Nyeusi music festival is all about promoting up and coming talent in the city. We want to bring people back that you don't see every year. It's fun, and it'll get people excited. Art is a big part of this city and Nyeusi is all about showcasing that. Here to create moments that last a lifetime. Good music, amazing energy, and beautiful people to uplift your soul. As from next year, we'll be auditioning new acts to headline alongside big acts. We believe some of the best talents out there are still hidden gems, and it's our goal to help unveil them. Please follow us on our social media for updates on the new acts auditions for next year if you believe you're one of the talents we're looking for.
